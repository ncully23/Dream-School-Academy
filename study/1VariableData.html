Basic Definitions & Concepts

  • Measures of Center: Mean, Median, Mode
  • Measures of Spread: Range, Interquartile Range (IQR), Standard Deviation (basic)
  • Percentiles & Quartiles: Q1 (25th percentile), Q2 (median), Q3 (75th percentile)
  • Interquartile Range (IQR): Q3 - Q1

Understanding Data Displays

  • Reading histograms, dot plots, and box-and-whisker plots
  • Identifying skewness (left-skewed, right-skewed, symmetric)
  • Understanding how skew affects the mean vs. median

Problem-Solving Skills

  • Calculating the Mean: Sum of values divided by count
  • Finding the Median: Order values and find the middle (or average of two middles)
  • Impact on Mean/Median: Understand how adding/removing values affects them
  • Using Frequency Tables: Compute mean/median using grouped data
  • Finding Missing Values: Use algebra to solve for missing data when mean/median is known
  • Weighted Averages: Account for different group sizes

Advanced Concepts

  • Standard Deviation Basics: Higher spread → higher SD
  • Transformations: Shifting/scaling data and its effect on SD
  • Comparing Distributions: Use mean and median positioning
  • Box Plots & Outliers: 1.5 × IQR Rule to find outliers

Key Skills

  • Frequency Tables
  • Mean, Median, Mode, Range, Standard Deviation
  • Box Plot Interpretation
