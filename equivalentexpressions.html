Equivalent Expressions

Core Algebra Skills

Properties of Exponents

  • Product Rule: \( x^a \cdot x^b = x^{a+b} \)
  • Quotient Rule: \( \frac{x^a}{x^b} = x^{a-b} \)
  • Power Rule: \( (x^a)^b = x^{a \cdot b} \)
  • Zero and Negative Exponents: \( x^0 = 1 \), \( x^{-a} = \frac{1}{x^a} \)

Combining Like Terms

  • Add or subtract polynomials by combining like terms

Factoring

  • GCF extraction
  • Trinomials: \( ax^2 + bx + c \)
  • Factoring by grouping
  • Difference of squares: \( a^2 - b^2 = (a - b)(a + b) \)

Expanding

  • Distributive property: \( a(b + c) = ab + ac \)
  • Squaring binomials: \( (a + b)^2 = a^2 + 2ab + b^2 \)
  • Cubing binomials and using special product formulas

Rational Expressions

  • Simplify by factoring
  • Use common denominators
  • Find undefined values in the denominator

Radicals & Fractional Exponents

  • \( \sqrt[n]{x} = x^{1/n} \)
  • Simplify square and cube roots
  • Rationalize denominators

Absolute Value

  • Simplify and evaluate absolute value expressions
  • Understand absolute value in equations and inequalities

Word Problem Translation

  • Convert verbal statements into algebraic expressions
  • Recognize equivalent forms in context (e.g., interest growth)

Functions and Substitution

  • Substitute and simplify expressions inside functions
  • Examples: \( f(x) + g(x) \), \( f(g(x)) \)

Algebraic Properties

  • Commutative: \( a + b = b + a \), \( ab = ba \)
  • Associative: \( (a + b) + c = a + (b + c) \)
  • Distributive: \( a(b + c) = ab + ac \)

SAT Strategies

  • Recognize common answer patterns
  • Match different forms (factored vs. expanded)
  • Simplify to match multiple choice answers
  • Identify structure to spot equivalency
  • Avoid common mistakes (exponent rules, factoring errors, sign errors)
