Right triangle \( \triangle ABC \) is shown. What is the value of \( \tan A \)?

A. \( \frac{\sqrt{3}}{54} \)
B. \( \frac{1}{\sqrt{3}} \)
C. \( \sqrt{3} \)
D. \( 27\sqrt{3} \)

ID: 5307879f Answer

Correct Answer: \( \sqrt{3} \)

Rationale

In right triangle \( \triangle ABC \), angle \( B = 30^\circ \) and hypotenuse \( AB = 54 \). Since angle \( A \) is adjacent to leg \( BC \) and opposite leg \( AC \), and the triangle is a 30-60-90 triangle, we can use trig ratios.

In a 30-60-90 triangle, the ratio of opposite to adjacent for angle \( A = 60^\circ \) is: \[ \tan A = \frac{\text{opposite}}{\text{adjacent}} = \sqrt{3} \] So, the correct answer is \( \sqrt{3} \).
